About the House

Rainbow Mansion is our 5,000 square foot home located in the heart of Silicon Valley.

Our house is centered around our well stocked library, our well stocked wet bar, our hardware lab, and our salon-hosting living room.

On a given night you may overhear an impromptu lecture on the history of navigation, be conscripted to play a newly invented variant of charades, or catch someone building a fireplace key using duct tape and a laser cutter.
